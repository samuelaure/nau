# naŭ — Viral Content Ideas (Feature-Driven)

> Content angles to promote the platform. Each feature gets multiple angles so the same capability becomes 3–5 different hooks.

---

## 1. Remotion Video Rendering (reels as code)

**The hidden truth:** competitors render with templates. naŭ renders with React components — every reel can be programmatically tweaked, batch-generated, and A/B tested.

- **Speed flex** — *"I made 30 reels in 4 minutes. Same brand, 30 hooks, 30 renders. Watch."* → screen recording of a single click producing a folder of MP4s.
- **The editor's nightmare** — *"Why I fired my video editor (and it's not what you think)."* → reveals it's not AI slop, it's deterministic code-based rendering.
- **Consistency obsession** — *"Your brand's reels look different every week. Here's why that's killing your growth."* → show 5 reels rendered with identical timing/typography/pacing.
- **The 1-pixel demo** — *"I changed one variable and re-rendered my entire backlog. Try doing that in CapCut."*

## 2. Competitor Idea Synthesis (pgvector + LLM)

**The hidden truth:** everyone says "study competitors." Nobody actually does it because transcribing 30 reels takes 6 hours. naŭ does it in 90 seconds and extracts the *idea DNA* — not the content.

- **Ethical theft** — *"How to steal from your competitors without getting sued."* → demo: paste @competitor handle → get 20 angles stripped of their brand voice.
- **The cringe reveal** — *"I fed my 3 biggest competitors into an AI. It found the pattern in their viral reels."* → show the synthesized pattern as a list.
- **Niche detective** — *"Why every fitness influencer opens with the same 4 words."* → use naŭ to surface the pattern publicly. Builds curiosity about the tool.
- **Reverse engineering** — *"I rebuilt @MrBeast's hook formula in a spreadsheet."* → naŭ does the work; you narrate the reveal.

## 3. Telegram Voice Journal → Triage

**The hidden truth:** ideas die in your Notes app. naŭ captures them where they're born — on the move — and routes them automatically.

- **The shower thought** — *"Where do your best ideas go to die? Mine used to. Then I built this."* → voice-note in shower → idea appears triaged into next week's calendar.
- **ADHD creator** — *"I have 400 voice memos in my phone. None became content. Here's the fix."*
- **The commute test** — *"30-minute drive. 8 reel ideas captured, drafted, and scheduled. I didn't touch my laptop."*
- **Inspiration vs. action** — split-screen: left = scrolling Instagram saving reels you'll never use; right = naŭ auto-filing them into InspoBase.

## 4. Calendar Auto-Fill / Scheduler

**The hidden truth:** posting cadence beats post quality for early growth. Most creators can't sustain cadence.

- **90 days in 9 minutes** — *"I filled my entire Q1 content calendar in one sitting. Here's the timelapse."*
- **The vacation flex** — *"I'm in Bali. My brand posted 14 reels this week. I didn't open my laptop."*
- **Cadence math** — *"Posting 5x/week vs. 1x/week: I tracked both for 60 days. The result wasn't what I expected."* — naŭ is what made 5x sustainable.
- **Multi-brand chaos** — *"How I run 4 Instagram brands without losing my mind."*

## 5. Brand Prompt Architecture (the 8-layer system)

**The hidden truth:** ChatGPT writes the same generic captions for everyone because everyone uses the same prompts. naŭ layers brand context so output is *yours*.

- **The ChatGPT problem** — *"Why your AI captions sound like a LinkedIn bro. And how to fix it in 5 minutes."*
- **Voice cloning, but for brands** — *"I trained an AI to write like my brand. Not me. My brand. Here's the difference."*
- **The taste layer** — *"Most AI tools have 1 prompt. Mine has 8. Here's why that matters."*

## Format Playbook (use across all of the above)

- **Screen-recorded "watch me do this in real-time"** — 30–60s, no edits. Highest conversion for SaaS demos.
- **Before/after split-screen** — old workflow (8 tabs, chaos) vs. naŭ (one screen).
- **Tier list / ranking** — *"I ranked every Instagram automation tool. naŭ won. Here's the criteria."* (self-aware, polarizing.)
- **Build-in-public teardown** — *"I scraped 1,000 viral reels. Here's what I found."* — naŭ is the implicit hero.
- **Carousel: "X things [creator type] does wrong"** — last slide = naŭ as the fix.
